✅ 4. Extra Notes for Writers (Technical Explanation)

Writers do not know ML, so give them this explanation:

⭐ Why TF-IDF was chosen

Simple, efficient, widely used for text classification

Works well for short emotional comments

Avoids giving too much importance to very common words

Helps the model focus on meaningful emotional keywords
(e.g., “hurt”, “sad”, “angry”, “panic attack”, “miss you”, etc.)

⭐ What "n-grams" means

unigram = one word → “fear”, “feel”, “crying”

bigram = two-word phrase → “panic attack”, “feeling lost”, “very sad”

Using bigrams improves emotion detection accuracy.

⭐ What “5000 features” means

The model chooses the top 5000 most important vocabulary items.
Each comment becomes a 5000-dimensional vector.

⭐ Why 80/20 stratified split

Ensures the test set contains equal proportions of each emotion type.
